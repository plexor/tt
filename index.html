The Adventures of Timmy the Tiger

Timmy the Tiger is a curious little tiger who loves to explore the forest. One day, he came across a babbling brook and couldn't resist the temptation to take a drink. As he leaned down to take a sip, he heard a strange noise coming from the other side of the brook.

Timmy sneaks over to see what the noise is.
Timmy runs away as fast as he can.
